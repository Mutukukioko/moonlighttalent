About Us

Welcome to AYC

The Akurinu Youth Convention (AYC) has been a beacon of empowerment and positive change for Akurinu youths since its inception in 1981. For over four decades, AYC has demonstrated an unwavering commitment to nurturing the potential, talents, and dreams of the youth within the Akurinu community.

The organization's dedication to empowering Akurinu youths is a testament to the power of community, unity, and the belief that every young person has the potential to make a meaningful impact.
