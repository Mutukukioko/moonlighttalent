Moonlight Talent

About Us

Moonlight Talent Group, born in 2018 as a self-help initiative by the vibrant youth of Moroto's Kadzandani Ward, emerged from the heart of the slum area. Our aim? To brighten our futures through the power of the arts.

Officially registered on March 5, 2019, our mission expanded to address pressing issues faced by young individuals, including sexual reproductive health and rights (SRHR) and the empowerment of young women and girls.

We believe in the transformative force of creative arts to spread our message. We are a dynamic self-help group committed to driving real change and empowerment among the youth in our community. Our unwavering mission is to cultivate and unleash the remarkable talents inherent in our local talent pool, be it in dance, acting, or poetry. By doing so, we are determined to provide a firm foundation for every mwananchi, enabling them to stand tall and make a meaningful impact within our community.
